About Me

I am a software engineer with a deep passion for systems level programming. I began my journey when I was 14 years old learning python, I soon taught myself C, C++, as well as Rust. Somewhere in high school I became interested in compiler engineering and ever since I have had the hobby of designing and implementing my own programming languages.

After high school I volunteered to serve in the United States Army as an infantryman. I served with the 25th Infantry Division in Hawaii and reached the rank of Specialist in a competitive unit. Due to an injury sustained in training I was medically discharged from the Army and given the opportunity to pursue my passion for software engineering.

I am now pursuing my B.S. in Computer Science at Western Governors University. I look forward to graduating and seeing what the future holds for me in the field of software engineering. I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow as a software engineer.
